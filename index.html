    Character Illustrations

    Congratulations to Courtney Cole of Guyer High School!

    As our character illustrator, she is drawing the story's characters - Ned, Cassi, and Issac - on a field trip around Denton County.

    Fall 2023

    Landmark Contest

    High school art students drew popular area landmarks and competed for their artwork to appear in the finished book.

    Scholarships and grants were awarded!
